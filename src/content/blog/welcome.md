Welcome to **Hartzog.ai** — a space dedicated to exploring data, technology, and ideas worth sharing.

## What to Expect

This site will be a growing collection of:

- **Blog posts** covering technology, data science, and research topics
- **Interactive data analysis** articles with live charts and explorable datasets
- **Educational resources** including books, papers, and reference materials

## Built for Growth

This site is designed to grow organically. New analysis articles can include interactive charts, filterable tables, and live data explorations — all rendered directly in the browser.

The resources section automatically picks up any books, PDFs, or documents added to the repository, making it easy to share educational materials.

Stay tuned for more content!
